The World We're Entering

The post-WWII financial order assumed economic interdependence would hold. For seventy years, it did. That assumption is now being tested.

Correspondent banking narrows as jurisdictions diverge. Cross-border settlement carries political risk it never carried before. Trillions in productive capacity — commodities, energy, infrastructure — sits locked in structures designed for a world of open capital flows.

The assets that underpin the real economy — commodities, energy, critical materials — represent a fraction of investable markets, yet demand for them is accelerating. This is a structural mispricing, and it will correct.

Meridian builds the infrastructure to make these assets liquid, collateralizable, and accessible to global capital — regardless of which correspondent banking relationships hold or fail.

How It Works
01

Tokenize

Wrap assets in compliant SPVs. Issue tokens as registered securities with legal claims on underlying cash flows.

02

Trade

Secondary markets with continuous price discovery. Same-day settlement. Liquidity where none existed before.

03

Collateralize

Post tokens to lending protocols. Borrow against holdings without selling. The lien is the code.

04

Stack

Combine tokens across jurisdictions into diversified collateral pools. No correspondent banking chains.

05

Swap

Atomic swaps between asset classes. Solar for copper. No intermediary, no counterparty risk.

06

Distribute

Programmatic distributions via smart contract. Cash flows hit, holders receive their share automatically.
